This website presents the stages of an IE 492 Graduation Project that was conducted in coordination with Nova Plumbing Solutions which operates in the sanitaryware sector.

Contributors

This project was carried out by Barış Burak Güneş, Çağla Yaren Aslan and Yasin Sarı under the supervision of Dr. Gönenç Yücel.

Project

The project was conducted in coordination with Nova Plumbing Solutions, a company that had difficulties in satisfying the orders on time.

Methodology

Aggregate production planning methodology was implemented. In this regard, a cost minimizing linear programming model was formulated.
